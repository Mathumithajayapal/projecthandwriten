Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping times?

Orders ship within 3-5 business days. US delivery takes 3-7 days, Canada 7-14 days. International shipping available upon request.

Return policy?

Unused, unopened products can be returned within 14 days. Candles and oils are final sale due to hygiene and safety standards.

Group workshop rates?

Yes! Groups of 6+ receive 15% off. Email us for custom corporate or private event quotes. We can host up to 20 guests.

Wholesale inquiries?

We partner with select boutiques and apothecaries. Please reach out via the contact form with "Wholesale" as subject, and include your store details.
